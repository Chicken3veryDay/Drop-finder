## Classification rules

- `identity_verification_required`: direct public evidence that identity or government-ID validation is required before access or purchase.
- `identity_verification_checkout_only`: direct public evidence of identity validation specifically during checkout.
- `self_attestation_21_plus`: a Yes/No, enter/exit, date, or checkbox age assertion without identity validation.
- `no_observable_age_gate`: the inspected public route showed no gate and the evidence was strong enough to make that limited observation.
- `uncertain`: client-side behavior, checkout-only behavior, inaccessible pages, or insufficient evidence prevents a stronger classification.

A disclaimer that products are for adults is not itself an age-verification mechanism. No vendor in this pass was upgraded to identity verification because no direct public evidence established that behavior.
